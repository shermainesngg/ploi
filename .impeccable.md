## Design Context

> Source of truth: the **PLOI Brand Guidelines V3**. Tokens live in
> `apps/web/src/app/globals.css` and `apps/web/tailwind.config.ts`. Logo components in
> `apps/web/src/components/ui/Logo.tsx` (`PloiLogo`, `PloiMark`, `PoweredByPloi`).

### Users
Three audiences on one platform, plus PLOI itself as the invisible infrastructure layer:
- **Customers** — Gen-Z fans (18–25) discovering local beauty/wellness services through creators
  they follow on TikTok/Instagram. Browsing on mobile in Bangkok, impulse-booking energy. They need
  to trust the page instantly — the creator recommendation is their trust anchor. They expect
  ClassPass/Klook-level booking UX.
- **Creators** — earn commission on attributed bookings; live in their dashboard checking earnings
  and link performance. Want clear proof of the value they drive.
- **Businesses** — adopt PLOI as a better booking system (the trojan horse); get creator
  attribution for free. Want a calm, reliable operations tool.

Bangkok-first (beauty & wellness), Singapore for initial testing.

### Brand Personality
**Commerce infrastructure for creator-driven discovery.** DISCOVER · BOOK · REWARD.
Three words: **invisible, trusted, creator-first.** PLOI is "the invisible layer that turns
recommendations into bookings and rewards creators for the value they drive." Confident and
understated — it recedes so the business and creator shine, asserting itself only where it adds
value (earnings, CTAs).

Two modes:
- **Mode 1 (PLOI-branded)** — marketing site + creator/business dashboards. Full PLOI lockup, ink-forward.
- **Mode 2 (invisible brand)** — the Shop Booking page (and customer-facing creator/confirmation
  pages). The business is the star; PLOI recedes to a quiet `PoweredByPloi` badge. No loud PLOI
  pills inside the business's page.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Klook meets ClassPass — clean activity-booking UX — but rebuilt on a refined, minimal,
  *infrastructural* identity. Lots of ink and warm-white breathing room; coral is rare and earns
  attention. Editorial, left-aligned, not centered-everything.
- **Theme**: light + dark both retained. Light = warm white ground (`#FAF9F6`), ink text. Dark =
  ink ground (`#0D1117`), warm white text. Coral brightens slightly in dark.
- **Palette** (60/30/8/2 is about visual *weight*, not pixel count):
  - Ink `#0D1117` — 60% (primary structural color, headings, primary CTAs)
  - Warm White `#FAF9F6` / Stone `#E8E4DE` — 30% (surfaces, borders)
  - Charcoal `#6B6B6B` — 8% (secondary text)
  - **Coral `#E05A47` — 2% system accent: actions, booking CTAs, earnings, highlights, positive states only.**
- **Typography** (from the brand guide — this overrides the impeccable skill's generic font advice):
  - **Plus Jakarta Sans** — primary. ExtraBold (800) headings/logo, Bold subheads, SemiBold
    buttons/prices, Regular body, Light captions.
  - **Space Mono** (`font-data`) — data & system: earnings, prices, IDs, attribution, timestamps.
- **Logo**: P-gem mark (a `P` whose counter is a faceted gem) + `PLOI` wordmark. One color, scales
  to any size.
- **Anti-references**: generic SaaS landing pages, bright-pink beauty apps, glassmorphic dashboards,
  bland white booking pages with blue CTAs. No purple/amber/cyan — everything resolves to the palette.
- **References**: Klook (bold trust badges, clean service cards, sticky CTA, "X booked today"),
  ClassPass (studio discovery, restrained accent, subtle elevation), Mindbody (booking-flow polish).

### Design Principles
1. **Coral is a guest, not the host.** Reserve coral for money, booking CTAs, and positive states.
   Structural CTAs (Sign up, Get started, primary `Button`) use ink — black on light, white on dark
   (the `--bridge-ink` token system). The coral booking action uses `Button variant="book"`. Never
   reach for coral just to add color.
2. **Be invisible when the work isn't yours.** On customer-facing business/creator pages, recede to
   `PoweredByPloi`. Loud PLOI branding belongs only in Mode 1 surfaces.
3. **Data wears Space Mono.** Earnings, prices, counts, IDs, timestamps render in `font-data` with
   tight tracking — it signals "this is real, measured value."
4. **Trust signals are first-class citizens.** "X booked this week", "Recommended by N creators",
   the creator attribution bar — visible without scrolling, as bold pill badges, not buried text.
5. **Warm, tinted neutrals — never pure black/white, never off-palette.** Everything resolves to
   ink, warm white, stone, charcoal, or coral.
6. **Mobile-native and fast.** Phone-first, 48px touch targets, sticky Book Now always visible. The
   Shop Booking page stays lean (minimal JS, no heavy animation).

### Shop Booking Page — Specific Direction
- **Hero**: bold trust pill badges (backdrop-blur over the business cover photo). "Instant Booking",
  "X booked this week". When no photo, a neutral ink gradient (`--bridge-hero-gradient`) lets the
  business own the frame.
- **Creator Bar**: creator attribution card right under the hero — the key trust differentiator (the
  creator IS the verification). Quiet, no PLOI pill; a chevron when it links to the source post.
- **Service Cards**: Klook-style left-right split — name/description/duration left, Space-Mono price
  + compact coral `Book` button right.
- **Sticky CTA**: fixed bottom bar, "Book Now" in coral (`variant="book"`), price in Space Mono.
- **Footer**: `PoweredByPloi` badge only — the single, quiet PLOI mark on the page.
