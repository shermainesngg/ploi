Key Constraints & Business Rules
  • Repeat-attribution lock. customer_acquisitions (customer_phone, business_id) is unique — one active acquisition per phone+business, with expires_at enforcing the 6-month residual window.
  • One link per pair. links (creator_id, business_id) is unique — a creator gets exactly one trackable link per business.
  • No double-counting payouts. payout_line_items (payout_id, booking_id) is unique.
  • One schedule row per weekday. staff_schedules (staff_id, day_of_week) is unique.
  • Junction PK. staff_services uses a composite primary key (staff_id, service_id) — the only table without a UUID id.
  • Cascade on ownership, restrict on money. services/links/staff cascade-delete from their owner; bookings FKs do not cascade, protecting financial records.
  • ⚠️ Missing FK. time_blocks.staff_id is a bare uuid with no foreign-key constraint to staff(id) — unlike bookings.staff_id. Likely an oversight.
